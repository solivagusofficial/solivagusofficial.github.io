Solivagus

Atmospheric Black Metal from the Amazon, Brazil

About

Solivagus is a solo atmospheric black metal project created by Flávio Dourado, vocalist and songwriter from Manaus, Brazil. Blending melancholic melodies, harsh vocals, and immersive ambient textures, Solivagus explores themes of solitude and existential despair.

The project seeks to bridge the raw emotion of black metal with the haunting beauty of the natural world — a deeply personal reflection about isolation, resilience, and transcendence amidst the vastness of the Amazon.

The Mind Behind

Flávio Dourado, 27, born in Manaus in Northern Brazil, is a veteran of the extreme metal scene of the Amazon region. Former vocalist of the thrash/death metal band Carnívoro, he is also a writer with two published books.
