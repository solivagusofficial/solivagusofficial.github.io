Solivagus

Atmospheric Black Metal from the Amazon, Brazil

About

Solivagus is a solo atmospheric black metal project created by Flávio Dourado in Manaus, Brazil. Rooted in the heart of the Amazon, the project explores melancholic landscapes, existential themes, and the tension between nature, memory, and spiritual erosion.
